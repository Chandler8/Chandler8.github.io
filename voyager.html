Space Voyager 3.0

Explore the Galaxy with this unique project.

Become a space voyager with this fun and interactive application! This website allows the user to register to become on official member of SV3. When the user registers it will then take them to the home page which will guide them to different sites. Sites including a page dedicated to life on Mars, a Planet Page which provides different information regarding the star, moon, & planets of our solar system, an ISS (international space station) Page that allows the user to view the location of where the space station is in real time, and the astronomy picture of the day (APOTD) powered by NASA.
